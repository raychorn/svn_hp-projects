Managing clusters

To access the cluster overview summary, select a cluster in the inventory tree, then click the HP Insight Management tab.

The Overview page for Cluster Management displays cluster information including Status summary.

Depending on the software installed, links may be available for the following management tools:

  • HP SIM

  • Onboard Administrator

  • HP OneView

For more information, see Navigating HP Insight Management with the Storage Module.
