HP SNMP agent and HP CIM provider events

HP Insight Control for vCenter registers with HP SNMP agents and HP CIM providers to receive traps from HP SNMP agents and indications from HP CIM providers as they occur. Events created based on these traps and indications fit into the defined vCenter Server categories.
