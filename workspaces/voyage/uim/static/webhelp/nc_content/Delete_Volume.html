Delete volume

An unassigned volume is a volume that is not associated with a datastore or RDM. You can delete unassigned storage volumes that belong to arrays that support provisioning.

To delete a volume:

  • If any errors are shown, address them and then retry the operation. Otherwise, click Delete to confirm the operation.

    The Storage Module for vCenter removes the LUN presentation from all hosts, deletes the volume, and re-scans the hosts. When these tasks are complete, the deleted volume is no longer displayed in the vSphere Client.

    TIP: Manual cache refresh is not necessary when the operation is finished. The cache will be refreshed automatically.
