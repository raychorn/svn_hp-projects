Refresh takes a long time to complete

In environments with HP EVAs, a cache refresh takes a long time.

Possible cause: A hardware failure on an HP EVA. Some hardware failures can negatively impact HP EVA Command View performance and lead to significantly longer refresh times by the Storage Module for vCenter.

Action: Remove or replace any failed drive on the HP EVA.
