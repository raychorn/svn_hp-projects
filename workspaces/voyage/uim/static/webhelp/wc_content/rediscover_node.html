Rediscovering devices

A rediscovery of all the devices associated with a selected host can be initiated using the Rediscover Node feature. The rediscovery process may take up to 15 minutes to complete. Information collected from the devices will be missing in various places in the plug-in until the rediscovery is complete.

To use this feature, select Rediscover Node from the Actions drop-down list.
